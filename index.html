Welcome to LuLu Belle Creations!

If you're looking for that unique handcrafted gift, then you've come to the right place. From various crocheted items to handmade jewlery, all of our products are made from high quality materials and are crafted in the USA.

We are based in Richardson, TX and currently only sell to customers located in Texas. Feel free to view our ever-growing catalog of products on our Facebook page. You can also find us at craft fairs in and around the Dallas/Fort Worth Metroplex. Need something that's custom made? Email us and we can give you a quote.
